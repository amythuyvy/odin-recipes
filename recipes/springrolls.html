Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Description

In Vietnamese, fresh spring rolls made with rice paper are called gỏi cuốn, translating to “salad rolls” (gỏi is means “salad” and cuốn means “to coil” or “to roll”).

Typically, you’ll find Vietnamese spring rolls filled with shrimp, pork vegetables, herbs and rice vermicelli. In this spring roll recipe, They primarily using shrimp, vegetables, herbs, and noodles for the filling. Feel free to add or replace ingredients based on your preferences and what’s available to you.

Ingredients

  • Rice Paper
  • Rice vermicelli
  • Shrimp
  • Lettuce Leaves
  • Vegetables and Herbs

Steps

Prepare the ingredients as neccessary

Rolling Technique for Vietnamese Spring Rolls

  1. Dip the rice paper in lukewarm water
  2. Add the filling
  3. Roll the spring roll
